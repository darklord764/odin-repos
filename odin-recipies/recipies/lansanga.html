Lasangna


Lasagna is a layered pasta dish with cheese, sauce, and meat or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried sage
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup cheese
  • 1/2 cup sauce
  • 1/2 cup onion
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup bacon
  • 1/2 cup chicken
  • 1/2 cup pork
  • 1/2 cup beans
  • 1/2 cup tomatoes

Directions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add ground beef and cook, stirring occasionally, until browned, about 10 minutes.
  3. Stir in butter and flour; season with salt, pepper, oregano, basil, thyme, parsley, and sage.
  4. Stir in milk, water, cheese, sauce, onion, mushrooms, bacon, chicken, pork, beans, and tomatoes.
  5. Cook, stirring occasionally, until sauce is thickened, about 15 minutes.
  6. Stir in remaining 1/2 cup cheese and sauce.
  7. Serve over rice.
  8. Enjoy!
