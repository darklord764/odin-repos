Butter Chicken


This is a simple recipe for making butter chicken. It's a great starter for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped garlic
  • 1/2 cup chopped ginger
  • 1/2 cup chopped cumin
  • 1/2 cup chopped coriander
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/2 cup chopped red chilli
  • 1/2 cup chopped green chilli

Instructions

  1. Gather all the ingredients
  2. Marinate the chicken with spices and yogurt
  3. Cook the marinated chicken in a pan
  4. Prepare the sauce and add the cooked chicken
  5. Simmer and serve with rice or naan
  6. Enjoy!
